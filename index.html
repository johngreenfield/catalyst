Help & Information

Welcome to Catalyst!

This application is designed to assist with executive function challenges. Here's a quick guide to the tools:

  • General Assistant: Ask any question for a helpful response.
  • Task Breakdown: Enter a large task to get smaller, manageable steps.
  • Brain Dump Organizer: Input a jumble of thoughts to get an organized list.
  • Tone Analysis: Understand the emotional tone of your text.
  • Formalizer: Rewrite text to be more formal or casual.
  • Meal Muse: List ingredients to get a recipe suggestion.
  • Time Estimator: Estimate how long a task will take to complete.
  • Deep Dive: Get a comprehensive exploration of a topic.

The Spoon or Energy Rating is based on Spoon Theory, a metaphor used to describe the limited amount of mental and physical energy a person has for daily activities, particularly for those with chronic illness or neurodivergence. In Catalyst, you can set your current energy level:

  • Average (3 Spoons): The tool provides detailed and comprehensive responses.
  • Low (2 Spoons): The tool provides simpler, more concise responses to reduce cognitive load.
  • Very Low (1 Spoon): The tool gives the most straightforward, easiest-to-process answers, ideal for when you're feeling overwhelmed.

This feature helps tailor the app's output to match your current capacity, making tasks more manageable.
